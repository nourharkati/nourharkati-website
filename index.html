North African artist, rooted in New York City. Music weaving the ancient and the modern. The string, sings alongside rhythms of yesterday and tomorrow. Carrying nostalgia, joy, and hope in every note.

Left home to find sound, wandering through Paris to New York, where the world shaped art and spirit.

Composing for film and performing on stage, with culture alive in every step, A simple truth guiding it all ‘Tunis in my mind.’
