نور حركاتي
music film shows press

Nour Harkati is a Tunisian musician blending North African traditions with modern sounds. Known for his cinematic style, his mastery of the Guembri and Oud adds ancient depth, while dynamic rhythms and reflective lyrics evoke nostalgia, joy, and hope.

At 23, he left Tunisia for Paris and New York, seeking self-discovery and refining his sound. Now leaning into music for film while continuing live performances, he remains deeply tied to his roots, sharing his culture with the heartfelt phrase: “Tunis in my mind.”
